Meet The Team

Sara Polon
writer & performer

Sara is an entrepreneur and the founder of Soupergirl, the DC-based fresh-food company she built from a farmers-market stand into a beloved local business. Before all that, she was a stand-up comic.

Now she's coming back to the stage with the story she spent two decades living — about denial, shame, a man named Macho Gazpacho, and the long, funny road to regretting none of it.

Anna Brosowsky
director & producer

Anna is director, producer and stage manager born and raised in Washington, D.C. She recently graduated from McGill University, where she served as President of the Arts Undergraduate Theatre Society, producing Great Comet, directing Company, and stage managing Hair and Legally Blonde. Back home, she trained at Studio Theatre and the Shakespeare Theatre Company, and helped lead the Teen Performance Company at Round House Theatre. She made her professional debut stage managing the world premiere of Webster's Bitch at Playhouse on Park in West Hartford, CT. Anna is a pro potluck-hostess and an amateur baseball-appreciator. She is thrilled to bring I Regret Nothing to the stage.
